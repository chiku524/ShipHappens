# Station Jobs

Station needs **7 of 10 jobs** completed to unlock the shuttle.

Job board shows **3 active jobs** at a time. Completing one spawns another.

**Fake-able jobs (Stowaway):** Manifest Lies, Paperwork Avalanche, Mop the Future.

---

## 1. Manifest Lies

**Zone:** Cargo Ring → Main Hub scan pad  
**Crew:** 1–2 · **Time:** ~90s · **Satisfaction:** +8 · **Fake-able:** Yes

1. Pull manifest at job kiosk → 3 SKUs listed
2. Find glowing crates in Cargo Ring
3. Drag to scan pad → beep + checkmark
4. Confirm at kiosk

**Fail:** Wrong crate honks and bounces off pad.

---

## 2. Crane of Regret

**Zone:** Cargo Ring  
**Crew:** 2–3 · **Time:** ~2–3 min · **Satisfaction:** +12

1. Enter crane seat (wobble physics)
2. Magnet pick crate
3. Move to 3 bay markers
4. Lower and release each

**Fail:** Drop = ragdoll bonk; miss marker = slime.

**Note:** Hardest job — build last.

---

## 3. Coolant Gargle

**Zone:** Ops Deck  
**Crew:** 2 · **Time:** ~75s · **Satisfaction:** +8

1. Connect hoses A–D to valves
2. Player A holds valves; Player B spins wheel
3. Pressure bar hits green zone
4. “Gargle” cue → confirm

**Fail:** Wrong valve = slime burp; red zone = floor slime 10s.

---

## 4. Dish Go Brr

**Zone:** Ops Deck exterior  
**Crew:** 2 · **Time:** ~90s · **Satisfaction:** +10

1. Climb wobbly ladder
2. Wheel player spins dish RPM meter
3. Ladder player micro-aligns reticle
4. Hold steady 5 seconds

**Fail:** Fall = ragdoll; over-spin launches props.

---

## 5. Paperwork Avalanche

**Zone:** Main Hub  
**Crew:** 1 · **Time:** ~60s · **Satisfaction:** +6 · **Fake-able:** Yes

1. Collect forms from printer
2. Feed pneumatic tube one at a time (5 total)
3. Return to kiosk

**Fail:** Feed self = sucked in 3s, spit out dizzy. Jam = 10s clear.

**Evidence:** Printer duplicate logs.

---

## 6. Power Hour

**Zone:** Ops Deck  
**Crew:** 1–2 · **Time:** ~45s · **Satisfaction:** +7

1. Read symbol order panel
2. Flip breakers in sequence
3. Hit master switch

**Fail:** Wrong breaker = 2s cartoon zap. Two wrong = lights out 15s.

---

## 7. Trust Fall Certification

**Zone:** Main Hub  
**Crew:** 3 · **Time:** ~60s · **Satisfaction:** +10

1. Volunteer on ledge
2. Fall emote / step off
3. Two catchers on safety mat within 1.5s
4. Repeat 3 times

**Fail:** Miss mat = HR Timeout respawn.

---

## 8. Vending Restock

**Zone:** Break Room  
**Crew:** 1–2 · **Time:** ~75s · **Satisfaction:** +6

1. Open vending panel
2. Load 3 snack crate types
3. Test vend

**Fail:** Wrong slot = snack projectile. Stowaway steals S2 here.

---

## 9. Duct Tape OR ELSE

**Zone:** Docking Arm  
**Crew:** 2 · **Time:** ~2 min · **Satisfaction:** +12

1. Tether to rail
2. Patch 5 hull holes (hold tape 3s each)
3. All 5 required before shuttle

**Fail:** No tether = fan blows you off. Unpatched = −10 Satisfaction.

---

## 10. Mop the Future

**Zone:** Station-wide  
**Crew:** 1 · **Time:** ~90s · **Satisfaction:** +5 · **Fake-able:** Yes

1. Equip mop (slow walk)
2. Clean 8 slime puddles
3. Return mop

**Fail:** Slip on puddle = ragdoll.

**Evidence:** “100% mopped” but puddles remain.
